Arduino is a hardware and software platform that lets users build interactive devices and applications. Arduino boards can read inputs from sensors and buttons, and then turn those inputs into outputs, like activating motors or turning on LEDs.

Breadboard

A breadboard is a plastic board with rows of holes where you can easily insert the leads of electronic components, allowing you to build and test circuits without soldering.

An IR sensor (infrared sensor) is an electronic device that detects infrared radiation, essentially "seeing" heat emitted by objects, allowing it to detect motion or the presence of objects without direct contact.
